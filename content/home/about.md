TillyHacks is a **48-hour invention marathon** for high schoolers in
the DMV. You team up, learn what you need to learn, and build
something real. Hardware, software, games, weird experiments — if you
can build it, you can bring it.

It's free, it's in Chantilly, and no prior experience is required.
If you've never been to a hackathon, this is the one to come to.
